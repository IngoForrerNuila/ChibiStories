MEET OUR CHIBI FRIENDS!

MICHI THE HOMELY KITTEN


Michi is a homely kitten, who quite likes the care that his family gives him. He is polite and friendly with his owners, however he has found very good friends in the surroundings of his house, which has made him go out more often than normal, thus living many adventures inside and outside his home. Something that he always has to deal with is his feline instincts which sometimes makes him have some misunderstandings or sometimes funny situations. He has a very close friendship with Leve and Fonce, who suddenly appeared in front of him without any explanation; two very funny birds with very opposite personalities.

Michi is an adult cat who has been well cared for and very well educated.

He is afraid of water and is sometimes intimidated by the dogs around his house.

Michi is a kitten who likes to eat a lot, sometimes that can bring him problems or some very funny situations.

Generally he loves to spend time at home sleeping or scratching the furniture in the house, however he is going out more often.

LEO THE GREAT PROTECTOR


Leo is a very friendly dog even though he is very disciplined and can sometimes get angry with others when he sees some prank at home. Leo is a very special canine since he was found wandering the streets when he was very young, taking shelter to protect himself from the inclement weather; unfortunately there are people who are not responsible with their pets who are friends of all of us human beings. Leo knows all our little friends very well, he is always watching and trying to take care of everyone at home and on many occasions accompanying our protagonists in his adventures.

Leo is an adult dog.

He also likes dog food very much, especially the bones with special flavors.

He likes to be outside a lot, as he is a very energetic dog.

LEVE THE BIRD OF LIGHT


Leve is a very sweet bird that likes to play, fly and discover many curious things in the patios of the houses of us humans. Fonce, who is her little brother, is always with her to protect her and live many adventures, however, both being very different in personality, they are united by a special bond. Mild likes to play with Michi a lot, even though unfortunately that can sometimes bother our beloved kitten since he often wants to be sleeping and resting at home. Leve and Fonce always pay visits to the rest of our friends like Ami our precious little mouse or Robin our traveling turtle or Bruce our dear nocturnal friend.

Leve is an infant bird.

He is afraid of water and is sometimes intimidated by the dogs around his house.

She likes that her brother Fonce de Ella accompanies her everywhere, they are basically an inseparable family.

FONCE THE BIRD OF COURAGE


Fonce is a very special bird, since he was born with unparalleled courage, he loves to accompany his sister Leve at all times and enjoy many adventures, at the same time he tries to teach her everything necessary for her to be independent. Fonce and Leve unfortunately are alone, however they have found a beautiful family at Michi and Leo's house. They have cute and friendly friends who are always aware of where they have gotten to, since they like their morning songs. They brighten the mornings of all families and that is why they always leave food and water for the two of them.

Fonce is an infant bird.

Fonce and Leve are expert singers and many times they whistle beautiful songs, many of which are played on the radios of nearby homes.

He likes to exercise, to fly for a long time, he likes adventures but even more he likes to protect her sister and teach her how to be independent, even though her personalities often impede her goal.

BRUCE THE NIGHT FRIEND


Bruce always has a story to tell, he always secretly observes how his friends have fun in the mornings, while he takes naps and sleeps with the relaxing song of Fonce and Leve. Bruce is a diurnal observer, he feeds on fruits and always plays with Michi and Leo, who are often awake at night. Everyone enjoys the stories that Bruce has to tell as there are many great adventures and some quite spooky ones at night. Amie is one of Bruce's best friends, they like to go for night walks together and reflect on life; They are both very intelligent beings who have managed to survive many situations throughout their lives and share many, especially the passion for fruits!

Bruce is an adult bat.

He likes to rest a lot in the morning, but in the afternoons and evenings he is very active.

He usually eats a lot at night and after that live many adventures with his friends, as long as they are awake.

When his friends sleep Bruce tries to watch his dreams, warning them of any danger showing the love he has for them.

AMIE THE FLOWER MOUSE


Amie is a little mouse who tends to collect flowers in the morning to decorate her nest, she always does that before enjoying her favorite fruit, her green apples! She always tends to accompany Leve on her adventures and protect Robin when she sneaks out of the house as she has a very great mother spirit. She seems to have a great affection for Bruce since he always brings some green apples whenever he can in sincere appreciation, besides Robin always tends to listen to her and also tell her many stories of those houses in which he has been. Amie always plays in the gardens near the flowers and on many occasions she has had to warn Leo of some pranks that the little ones do inside or outside the house, she has matured a lot despite her youth and has learned of the beauties and dangers that they can expect inside or outside the houses, advising in this way the most adventurous of the home.

Amie is a teenage mouse.

She enjoys everyone's company, however, on many occasions she tends to spend many hours taking care of her nest, making it look beautiful even to the eyes of humans!

Michi is a kitten who likes to eat a lot, sometimes that can bring him problems or some very funny situations.

The most beautiful flowers are always a delight to observe, being even more beautiful to see how they adorn her nest and sometimes hers more beautiful dresses!

ROBIN THE ADVENTUROUS TURTLE


Robin is a tortoise that loves to leave home, even though he often enjoys hiding under sofas or beds, he tends to run away from home to enjoy the garden and bravely beyond it. Leo always meets Robin, correcting his direction and returning him home due to his youth and how dangerous it can be to go beyond the garden. Robin enjoys eating fish very much but there is something more pleasant for him and that is to be under the sunlight and a lot of water around. Robin was found by the family some time ago lonely near a river, however his small size alerted the family to rescue him and give him a momentary home. He really gets along very well with everyone even though sometimes he has escaped and disappeared for days... that same thing happens with Amie on some occasions, could it be that she also meets Robin?

Robin is a teenage turtle.

He tends to go all over the house and find secret passageways which he occupies to go out to the garden and sometimes visit more distant places to live many fun adventures and others not very safe.

At home he has water and food, however we all know that food tastes much better outdoors; why not enjoy some fallen fruit from the trees?
