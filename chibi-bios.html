MEET OUR CHIBI FRIENDS!

MICHI THE HOMELY KITTEN


Michi is a homely kitten, who quite likes the care that his family gives him. He is polite and friendly with his owners, however he has found very good friends in the surroundings of his house, which has made him go out more often than normal, thus living many adventures inside and outside his home. Something that he always has to deal with is his feline instincts which sometimes makes him have some misunderstandings or sometimes funny situations. He has a very close friendship with Leve and Fonce, who suddenly appeared in front of him without any explanation; two very funny birds with very opposite personalities.

Michi is an adult cat who has been well cared for and very well educated.

He is afraid of water and is sometimes intimidated by the dogs around his house.

Michi is a kitten who likes to eat a lot, sometimes that can bring him problems or some very funny situations.

Generally he loves to spend time at home sleeping or scratching the furniture in the house, however he is going out more often.
